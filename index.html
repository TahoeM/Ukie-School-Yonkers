About Us

Our Saturday Ukrainian School offers children the opportunity to immerse themselves in Ukrainian language, culture, and traditions. Our mission is to preserve and promote Ukrainian heritage through educational programs for children and youth.

Our Programs

  • Language Classes: Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced levels for all age groups.
  • Cultural Activities: Ukrainian folk dancing, music, history lessons, and art.
  • Special Events: Cultural performances, Ukrainian holidays, and more!
